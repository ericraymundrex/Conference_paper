2022 IEEE International conference on Recent Advances in Electrical, Electronics and Computer Engineering

ICRAEECE 2022

About the conference

The scope of the conference includes recent advances in the area of energy, electric vehicles, smart grid, recent communications, Artificial intelligence, machine learning, big data and IoT. The conference aims at new innovative research and industry standard works to be discussed and presented by the authors. This platform will pave the way for young researches to understand the depth of work in their area and hence support them to formulate their objectives. In addition, the discussion among the engineers will find a way for many innovation in the research works.

Mode of the conference

Due to the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic, ICECCT 2021 will be held in HYBRID MODE. Authors can present their paper on their convenient choice either in Virtual mode or physical mode.

Publications

Proceedings of ICECCT 2021 will be forwarded to be published in IEEE Xplore, a digital library indexed in Scopus, Web of Science, etc. ISBN of the proceedings of ICECCT 2021 are 978-0-7381-4415-3 (Xplore Compliant) and 978-0-7381-4414-6 (Print). ICECCT is pleased to inform that the proceedings of its previous all three editions (2015, 2017 and 2019) have been published in the IEEE Xplore. There had been more than 3,000 manuscripts received by the ICECCT conference Secretariat and 799 manuscripts had been registered and presented in the previous three editions of ICECCT and published in IEEE Xplore. The acceptance ratio of ICECCT is less than 30. Participants from more than 30 countries visited the ICECCT conference venue and presented their research articles.

How to submit your paper?

Full length manuscripts (in pdf) shall be submitted to icecct2021@gmail.com. In response to the numerous requests from the prospective authors, the deadline for manuscript submission has been EXTENDED to 15, July 2021 (Final Call).
